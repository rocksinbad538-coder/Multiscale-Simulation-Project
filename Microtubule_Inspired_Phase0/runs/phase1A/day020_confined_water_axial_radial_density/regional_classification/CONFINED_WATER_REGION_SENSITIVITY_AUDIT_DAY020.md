# Day020 Confined-Water Region Sensitivity Audit

The regional partition was evaluated for nine combinations of interfacial half-width and mouth half-width.

| Region | Central count | Count range | Fraction range | Relative span |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Interior core | 454.597 | 399.423–505.497 | 7.664–9.699% | 23.334% |
| Interfacial shell | 448.014 | 353.302–558.421 | 6.779–10.715% | 45.784% |
| Mouth zones | 108.870 | 86.796–141.860 | 1.665–2.722% | 50.578% |
| Exterior | 4200.287 | 4114.815–4266.173 | 78.952–81.857% | 3.604% |

## Interpretation

Large variation in a regional count indicates that the result depends strongly on the operational boundary. Such a region should not be reported as a unique physical population until the density profiles are used to identify structural minima.
