# Day020 Combined-Mechanism Figures

## Accepted quantitative ranges

- PYR4 gateway fraction: 0.842996 to 0.914134.
- Final PYR5 population at 100 ps: 0.000160 to 0.110107.
- Final PYR5 population for gamma_phi=100 ps^-1: 0.105186 to 0.110107.
- Stationary PYR5 population: 0.241117 to 0.999977.

## Figure files

1. `fig_day020_final_PYR5_vs_dephasing`: transient PYR5 population at 100 ps.
2. `fig_day020_steady_PYR5_vs_dephasing`: competition between thermal relaxation and local dephasing in the stationary state.
3. `fig_day020_PYR4_gateway_vs_dephasing`: robustness of PYR4 as the dominant gateway.
4. `fig_day020_representative_PYR5_time_traces`: representative transient dynamics for T=300 K and kappa=1 ps^-1.

Each figure is written in PNG and PDF formats.

## Interpretation boundary

The absolute dissipative rates are phenomenological sensitivity parameters. The figures support relative mechanistic conclusions but not microscopic relaxation times derived from a spectral density.
