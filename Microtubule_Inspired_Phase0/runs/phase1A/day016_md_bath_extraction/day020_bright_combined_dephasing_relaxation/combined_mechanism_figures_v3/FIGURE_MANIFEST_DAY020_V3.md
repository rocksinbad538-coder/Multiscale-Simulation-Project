## Statistical conventions

- Population curves: pooled descriptive standard deviation.
- Final populations: descriptive standard deviation over 63 realizations.
- Stationary populations: descriptive standard deviation over 21 snapshots.
- Gateway uncertainty: 95% bootstrap confidence interval for the aggregate ratio of fluxes, with snapshots as the resampling unit.
- The dephasing-rate axis uses a symmetric-log scale to represent zero and the physical values 1, 20, and 100 ps^-1.
