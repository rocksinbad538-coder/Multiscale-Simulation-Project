## Statistical conventions

- Main panel (a): pooled ensemble mean and population SD across 21 Hamiltonian snapshots and three high-state initial conditions.
- Main panel (b): mean and population SD across 63 frame–initial-state realizations.
- Main panel (c): mean and population SD across 21 stationary states.
- Supplementary gateway figure: flux-weighted mean and weighted SD across 63 frame–initial-state contributions.
- Dephasing axes use the physical values `0, 1, 20, 100 ps^-1` on a symmetric-log scale with a linear region around zero.
