## Initial Role

This macOS environment will be used for:

- project organization;
- documentation;
- geometry generation;
- small test systems;
- scripting;
- analysis;
- plotting;
- visualization;
- preparation of inputs for larger Linux/HPC simulations.

## Larger Simulations

Large-scale production simulations may be migrated later to a Linux workstation or HPC/cloud environment depending on system size, trajectory length, number of replicas, and computational cost.
