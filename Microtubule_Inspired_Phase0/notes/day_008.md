# Day 008 — Lead hybrid reproducibility and Phase 1 preparation

## Objective

Consolidate the carved 12-dipole chromophore-bearing BN-like scaffold-water model as a reproducible Phase 0 lead hybrid candidate and begin Phase 1-oriented analysis preparation.

## Starting point

Day 007 established the carved 12-dipole BN-like hybrid as the current lead candidate:

- stable field-free to 20k
- stable under fieldZ to 30k
- radial confinement preserved
- field-induced axial water-dipole response observed
- final 30k fieldZ response: mean cos(theta_z) ≈ 0.662 and S_z ≈ 0.342

## Day 008 plan

1. Run an independent field-free replicate of the carved hybrid candidate.
2. Compare replicate confinement and dipole behavior against the Day 007 field-free trajectory.
3. If stable, proceed to either a short fieldZ replicate or a weaker-field diagnostic.
4. Start Phase 1-ready analysis preparation:
   - water residence/contact near chromophore pseudo-sites
   - dipole autocorrelation/relaxation
   - representative-frame selection for later electronic parameterization
